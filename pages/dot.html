Infinite Generating Terrain

Inspired by CodingTrain, I'm building an infinite terrain generator in  Python,

utilizing  Perlin noise  (for procedural randomness) and  Tkinter  (GUI).

So far..

-I Made 30x20 field of rectangles split diagonally, creating 1200 triangles to later simulate elevation.

-By applying  isometric projection, I was able to display the peaks and give the 2D terrain a 3D appearance.

But I still need to..

-Optimize the rendering to reduce GPU load.

-Scale the camera  to create the illusion of flying through infinite terrain.
